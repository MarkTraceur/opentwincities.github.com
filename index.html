Warm Weather Hacking Coming to the Twin Cities

May and June will be busy months for civic hackers in the Twin Cities. On May 25th, civic hackers and neighborhood leaders will be at the Downtown Minneapolis Library for Visualizing Neighborhoods: A Hackathon for Good. A week later, on June 1st and 2nd, DevJam and Open Twin Cities will be taking part in National Day of Civic Hacking.
