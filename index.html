4th Tuesdays at Free Geek

We'll be hacking away at Free Geek Twin Cities on the 4th Tuesday of every month. Show up anytime between 5:30pm and 8pm. RSVP for meetings: May 28, 2013, June 25, 2013

Warm Weather Hacking Coming to the Twin Cities

May and June will be busy months for civic hackers in the Twin Cities. On May 25th, civic hackers and neighborhood leaders will be at the Downtown Minneapolis Library for Visualizing Neighborhoods: A Hackathon for Good. A week later, on June 1st and 2nd, DevJam and Open Twin Cities will host Hack for MN, the Twin Cities' verison of the National Day of Civic Hacking.
